What's new

Chatter can create hyperlinks for urls when sent in a chat message.

Included modules

SessionMonitor
This will watch your session for the inactivity prompt and attempt to prevent Plato from automatically logging you out. Useful for a personal computer that stays turned on most of the time and you wish to remain logged in.
Chatter
This will provide all participants of a chat with a unique color and style the chat with message bubbles, increasing the ability to read and follow the chat. In addition, this will add an option to allow the Enter key to send your message, rather then having to click the "Send" button for every message.
Note: all modules can be enabled and disabled as desired.
